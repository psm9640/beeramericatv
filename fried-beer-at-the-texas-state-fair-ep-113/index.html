Fried Beer at the Texas State Fair, Ep. 113

Posted by Paulon October 17, 2010 |

The Texas beer scene has been less than unique or exciting, but with a quick trip to the 2010 Texas State fair in Dallas, that all changed! If you haven’t heard about it yet, fried beer has hit the scene, and this week, I’m going to try it! I know the motto of our show is that “taste is subjective”, but all I have to say to you before you watch this vlog is, try making pretzel dough into the shape of a ravioli, fill it with beer, fry it up, add a little salt and pop it in your mouth – what do YOU think it will taste like? Cheers…sort of.
