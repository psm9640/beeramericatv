Beer America TV Brewing Network Segment

Posted by Paulon April 1, 2009 |
Beer America TV on the Brewing Network [ 14:06 ] Download

It was fun had by all, and the Brewing Network was kind enough to have BeerAmerica.TV on board for their Sunday Session on March 22, 2009. It was a 4 hour affair featuring our own John Pinkerton, David Little, Paul Leone and Chris Lady
. Most of the talk revolved around Moon River Brewing and brewing techniques, but if you just want to hear the segment of BeerAmerica.TV, I have it for you here.

If you’d like to hear the whole thing, go to the Brewing Networks website – http://www.thebrewingnetwork.com/ –  and download the whole episode. A warning that the language is unedited, so be prepared.

Many thanks again to the Brewing Network, who does a great job bringing brewing entertainment and info to the Craft beer loving universe!
