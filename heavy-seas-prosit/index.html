Heavy Seas, Prosit! – Ep. 112

Posted by Paulon October 3, 2010 |

OK, in October, it seems everyone is coming out with an Octoberfest of some sort, well not Heavy Seas
. This week we dive into a serious dose of PROSIT! by Clipper City Heavy Seas series. This is a full bodied malty glass full of goodness, with serious a backbone of grains and a few hops thrown in for good measure! Besides good info on this beer, John Pinkerton lets you in a on a little secret on what you can find in the Hofbrauhaus bathrooms!
