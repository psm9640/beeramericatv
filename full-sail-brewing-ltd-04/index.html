Full Sail Brewing: LTD 04, Ep. 158

Posted by Paulon January 23, 2012 |

Most of the time here on Beer America TV we discuss beers full of malt, tons of hops or a combination of both, this week, we discuss a beer thats basic, but still full of flavor
. Full Sail LTD 04 is a beer brewed with only a single malt and a single hop, and it’s perfectly balanced! Its golden deliousness weighs in at a 7% ABV and is very sessionalble – check it out!
