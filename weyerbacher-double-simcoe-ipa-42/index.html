Weyerbacher Double Simcoe IPA – Episode 42

Posted by Paulon February 27, 2009 |

If you’re afraid of the bitter beer taste that IPA’s sometimes carry but still love a big hoppy beer, Weyerbacher Brewing Company has a solution for you with their Double Simcoe! Simcoe is a hybrid hop variety that was developed in 2000 by Select Botanicals Group LLC to provide great hop flavor without the harsh aftertaste . We were certainly surprised by this excellent brew!
