A Candid Conversation with Brooklyn Brewery’s Garrett Oliver – Ep. 59

Posted by Paulon May 29, 2009 |

We all know Garrett Oliver is the best dressed man in the brewing business, but he’s also the brewer of some pretty great beers as well
. As Brewmaster of the Brooklyn Brewery, Garrett knows quite a bit about American Craft beer as well as food. We caught up with him at the Craft Brewers Conference and let him talk about anything he wanted. This raw and somewhat candid interview is worth listening to from beginning to end! He has no problem letting us know about the progress and problems his industry faces and wants you to know about it!
