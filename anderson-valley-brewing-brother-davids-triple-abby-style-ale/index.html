Anderson Valley Brewing: Brother David’s Triple Abby Style Ale, Ep. 125

Posted by Paulon January 31, 2011 |

In honor of our good friend and contributing vlogger David Little, John and Paul talk about Brother David’s Triple Abby Style Ale from the Anderson Valley Brewing Company located in Boonville, California. This Triple is complex, but well balanced with a strong malt backbone blended with spicy hops! The 10% ABV isn’t upfront, which is always a good thing . Since moving to Texas, David hasn’t been able to be a part of the Beer America TV vlogs, so here’s to you in spirit.
