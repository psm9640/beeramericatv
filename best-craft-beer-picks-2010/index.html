Best Craft Beer Picks 2010, Ep. 122

Posted by Paulon December 30, 2010 |

It’s that time of year when Beer America TV brings you our favorite beers of 2010 . This is our third year doing this and it gets tougher every year for sure. Our picks are not similar at all, but they are both tasty and worth trying if you ever get the chance! We wish all of you a happy 2011, may it better than 2010, we appreciate all of you for your emails, comments, and suggestions! We hope for bigger things ourselves in 2011, so say a prayer, and keep supporting your local brewery and beyond every chance you get!
