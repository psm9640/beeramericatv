Interview with Bob Cannon, Boston Brewery, Ep.57

Posted by Paulon May 17, 2009 |

You might not know his name, but if you’ve seen any commercials about Sam Adams, you’ve certainly seen his face . Bob Cannon is a brewer at the Boston Beer Company and has somewhat of a cult following in New England. He was nice enough to give Beer America TV a few minutes of his time at the 2009 Craft Brewers Conference.
