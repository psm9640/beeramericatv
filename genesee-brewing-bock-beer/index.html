Genesee Brewing: Bock Beer, Ep. 142

Posted by Paulon July 19, 2011 |

There is nothing better to the Beer America TV team of Paul Leone and John Pinkerton then receiving a package of beer in the mail. Even better is when one of our viewers sends it!! This week we discuss Genesee Bock Beer thanks to our good friend Brian Stiehler from Rochester New York . As part of their Heritage Series, the Genesee Brewing company has been brewing this Bock since the late 1800’s and after a bit of a hiatus, brought it back in 1951 and has been making it ever since. The can is awesome and the beer inside is damn tasty, so sit back and relax and enjoy this weeks episode! Thanks again Brian!!
